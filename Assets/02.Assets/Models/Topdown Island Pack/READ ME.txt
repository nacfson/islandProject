Thank you for purchasing the pack!

The pack use the universal render pipeline and post effects (already setup in the main camera)

There is a demo scenes and prefabs folder for each assets. You can use this as a starting point for your own creations

There are 2 rigged characters with 3 skins each and accessories (character controller are not included)
There is also a bird model with 3 animations and skins.

The terrain and buildings assets are modular and will snap with each other or on a grid (hold "V"or"ctrl") to do so)

There are several props, plants, crops, etc.. to decorate your levels.

As well as several custom effects and shaders (butterlies, smoke, clouds, water, terrain triplanar, foliage wiht wind,etc..)
